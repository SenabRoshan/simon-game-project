Simon Game

How to Play !!!

  • Watch the sequence of colors lighting up.
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the buttons in order.
  • If correct, a new color is added, lighten up in the next round.
  • The sequence gets longer and complex with each level.
  • One mistake ends the game.

    • This game boosts memory and focus while enhancing brain adaptability and muscle memory.
